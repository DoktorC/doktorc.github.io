About

Hi! I am Lorenzo Casalino, a former Ph.D. student at CEA-List, in Grenoble (FR). Across my Ph.D. I investigated the impact of CPU microarchitectures on the practical security of masked software implementations and compiler-based methodologies to automate the generation of micro-architecturally secure masked software implementations.
